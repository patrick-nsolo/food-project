    About The Seafood House

    The Seafood Restaurant:
    A Haven for Seafood Lovers.
    At The Seafood Restaurant, we believe that the ocean's bounty should be savored, enjoyed, and celebrated. Our restaurant was born out of a passion for seafood and a desire to share that passion with others. We are committed to providing our customers with the freshest, most delicious seafood available and an unparalleled dining experience.

    The Founder

    Jean-Louise Fournier was a man with a passion for cooking and a love of the sea. He grew up in a small coastal town in France, where he spent his childhood exploring the beaches, fishing with his father, and helping his mother in the kitchen. As he got older, Jean-Louise developed a talent for cooking seafood and dreamed of opening his own restaurant where he could share his love of seafood with others.
    After completing culinary school in Paris, Jean-Louise spent several years working in some of the city's top restaurants, honing his skills and learning new techniques. But he never forgot his dream of opening his own seafood restaurant by the sea. So, he returned to his hometown and started searching for the perfect location.
    Finally, in the summer of 2017, Jean-Louise opened the doors to his new restaurant, The Seafood House. He was determined to create a restaurant that celebrated the best of French seafood cuisine, using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients and preparing them with his own unique flair.
    The Seafood House continued to grow and flourish, thanks to Jean-Louise's unwavering commitment to quality and his love of seafood.
